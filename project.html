OUR PROJECTS

Changing Lives Through Compassion and Action

At HAYAT AID ORGANIZATION, we don't just talk about making a difference — we do it.

Every project we run is a direct response to the needs of the communities we serve in Iganga and surrounding areas.

01.

Orphan Feeding Program

Overview

Every child deserves a full stomach and a hopeful heart. Through this program, we provide regular nutritious meals to orphans and vulnerable children in Iganga and surrounding rural areas. Special emphasis is placed during Ramadan and Eid, where we deliver hot meals daily throughout the holy month.

Impact

  • Fed over 800 orphans in Ramadan 2024 alone
  • Supported 5+ local orphan centers and villages
  • Encouraged school attendance by alleviating hunger
  • Restored hope and dignity to children through consistent care
02.

Qurban Sacrifice & Meat Distribution

Overview

During Eid al-Adha, we perform Qurban sacrifices on behalf of donors and distribute fresh meat to families who rarely, if ever, eat meat. The process is done in accordance with Islamic principles and reaches the most remote and needy areas..

Impact

  • Over 100 animals sacrificed in 2023
  • More than 2,500 individuals received meat
  • Beneficiaries include widows, orphans, and elderly households
  • Reconnected remote Muslims with the joy of Eid
03.

Quran Distribution Initiative

Overview

We distribute printed copies of the Holy Quran to madrasas, newly converted Muslims, mosques, and Quran students in underserved areas. Many of these communities have just one or two shared Qurans — some handwritten or torn.

Impact

  • Over 3,000 Qurans distributed since 2022
  • Supplied Qurans to 30+ learning centers and mosques
  • Improved access to Islamic education for children and adults
  • Empowered da'wah and personal spiritual growth
04.

Orphan Feeding Program

Overview

We construct fully functional mosques in villages where Muslims have no proper place to pray. Each mosque includes ablution areas, prayer halls, and often solar lighting.

Impact

  • 6 complete mosques built in different districts
  • Facilitated daily prayers, Friday sermons, and community gatherings
  • Benefitted 5,000+ Muslims with accessible worship space
  • Created centers of peace, learning, and unity
