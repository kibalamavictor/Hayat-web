Our Projects

Changing Lives Through Compassion and Action

We are a non-profit organization that started with a deep conviction to make a real impact in communities. Our mission is to alleviate suffering, promote education, and foster sustainable development.

Through dedicated efforts and the generous support of our donors, we strive to bring about lasting change and hope where it's needed most.

01.

Orphan Feeding Program

Overview

The Orphan Feeding Program aims to provide nutritious meals to orphans, vulnerable children, and needy families, ensuring food security and promoting healthy development.

We provide comprehensive feeding programs daily through our various initiatives, ensuring no child goes hungry.

Impact

  • Reached 5,000 orphans in Eastern Uganda annually.
  • Improved nutritional status and health of children.
  • Reduced school absenteeism due to hunger.
  • Built stronger ties within communities through consistent meals.
02.

Qurban Sacrifice & Meat Distribution

Overview

During Eid al-Adha, we perform Qurban sacrifices on behalf of donors and distribute meat to families who cannot afford it.

The program is done in partnership with Islamic scholars to ensure the most compassionate and Islamic way.

Impact

  • Over 800 families received meat in 2024.
  • More than 5,000 individuals received fresh, high-quality meat.
  • Reduced food insecurity during festive seasons.
  • Provided valuable spiritual benefits to the givers.
03.

Quran Distribution Initiative

Overview

We distribute printed copies of the Holy Quran to individuals, newly converted Muslims, mosques, and Quran students in underserved rural areas.

Many of these communities have just one or two copies or none — some have never seen one.

Impact

  • Over 4,000 Qurans distributed since 2020.
  • Supplied Qurans to 30+ learning centers and mosques.
  • Improved access to Islamic education for children and adults.
  • Promoted spiritual growth.
04.

Masjid Construction Projects

Overview

We construct fully functional mosques in villages where Muslims have no proper place to pray.

Each mosque includes ablution areas, prayer halls, and often solar lighting.

Impact

  • 5 complete mosques built in different districts.
  • Facilitated daily prayers, Friday sermons, and community gatherings.
  • Benefitted 5,000+ Muslims with accessible worship spaces.
  • Created centers of peace, learning, and unity.
